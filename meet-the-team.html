Derek Buzasi
Whitaker Eminent Scholar in Science

Derek studies stars of all kinds, focusing primarily on stellar activity and oscillations. He also has been known to dabble in exoplanet detection, stellar-planetary interactions, and astronomical instrumentation both ground and space-based. Currently he’s leading a design study for MAGIC, a multichannel NUV SmallSat which will detect and characterize oscillations in massive stars.

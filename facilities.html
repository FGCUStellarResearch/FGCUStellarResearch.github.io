Facilities

SARA Observatory

Our institution is a member of the SARA (The Southeastern Association for Research in Astronomy) Consortium. Instruments used include SARA-KP and SARA-CT.

Apache Point Observatory

Dr. Derek Buzasi is a leasing partner for the Apache Point Observatory. Instruments used include the 3.5 meter and the 0.5 meter.

TESS Science Support Center

Dr. Derek Buzasi and students who are an active part of his project have had the opportunity of studying data sent back from TESS (Transiting Exoplanet Survey Satellite).

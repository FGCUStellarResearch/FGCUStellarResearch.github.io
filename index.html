About Us

We are a group of researchers using ground and space-based facilities to conduct astronomical research generally centered around stellar astrophysics. This includes activity in solar-like stars and active binaries, gyrochronology and stellar rotational evolution, stellar oscillations of all types, polarimetry, and stellar atmospheres. We are heavily involved with the development and deployment of the TASOC pipeline for the TESS mission. Sometimes we also dabble in non-stellar science (like exoplanet detection and gamma ray bursts!), instrumentation projects for ground and space, and even a little computational modeling and theory.
